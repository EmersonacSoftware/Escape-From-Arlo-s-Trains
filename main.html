About The Game
"Escape From Arlo's Trains", also known as "EFAT" is Emersonac Software's most recent game, released under the new Emersonac Interactive umbrella. This one-of-a-kind browser-hosted game is our first attempt at a website game host.
Sometime in the near future, Lego Island is invaded by Arlo Stans from another world, hellbent on revenge against mankind for oppressing Arlo! Fight your way to destiny as the Arlo stans prepare to attack your channel and your reputation, through Arlo Stans, Tweets and even Arlo's own creatures to defeat the evil Arlo boss once and for all! Take the world's fate in your hands in this modern browser-playable game!
Features:
- Minutes of action-packed gameplay!
- 5 Levels + Final Boss!
- Smooth Controls!
- 3 Lives!
- Bosses: Watch out, these things are serious!
- Sattelite Weapons: hook 'em up, use when you need 'em!
- Achievements!
- Over 40 Minutes of CI4 soundtrack!
- Customizable Loadout: Buy skins/weapon types with points!
- Custom Sprites!
